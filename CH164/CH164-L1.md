## Sizes, scales, and units
### Lengthscales
- Nuclei are $2*10^{-15}$ m, or 2 **femtometres** (fm). 
- Atoms are about **50000x larger**, at $1*10^{-10}$ m, also written as 100 **picometres** (pm), or 1 **angstrom** ($\mathring{A}$).
